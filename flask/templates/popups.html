About

FINN is an AI-driven stock management assistant designed to simplify personal finance and empower informed investment decisions. Utilizing advanced AI and machine learning, FINN provides personal financial advice, and real-time market insights.

It helps users navigate financial markets by offering guidance. FINN continuously adapts to market dynamics, delivering timely stock trends and news for proactive decision-making. Its robust features include portfolio tracking, performance analytics, and stock screening, all accessible through a user-friendly interface.

Catering to both individual investors and financial advisors, FINN ensures data accuracy and security, supporting users in achieving financial wellness and success.
